Yummy Pho

Description

This is a Vietnamese soup that is hearty and satisfying. It is composed of broth, rice noodles, mung bean sprouts, onions, cilantro, and a slice of lime. Typically, it will be a beef-based broth with beef, but you can use any type of broth and your choice of protein.

Ingredients

  • your choice of homemade or store-bought broth, i.e. beef, chicken, or vegetable broth
  • your choice of sliced or shredded protein, i.e. beef, chicken, or tofu
  • rice noodles
  • fish sauce or tamari sauce
  • mung bean sprouts
  • sliced onion
  • basil, thai basil preferably but any basil will work
  • cilantro sprigs
  • slices of lime
  • your choice of sauces, i.e. hoisin, sriracha, etc.

Steps

  1. Heat up your broth in a stockpot along with your sliced onion. Add a couple dashes of fish sauce to the broth.
  2. Soak your rice noodles in hot water in a bowl for 20 minutes.
  3. Then add your rice noodles into the stockpot along your meat to heat it up for a few minutes.
  4. Portion your noodles and meat into bowls and add the sprouts, basil, cilantro, and lime to each bowl.
  5. Add in any hoisin or sriracha sauce to your liking.
